Buffo

Buf­fo Introduction

Buf­fo is a spir­it­ed, the­atri­cal type­face that em­bod­ies the essence of com­e­dy and caprice. Draw­ing in­spi­ra­tion from the comedic op­er­at­ic term buf­fo, this font bursts onto the scene with ex­ag­ger­at­ed flair, be­com­ing the star of the show. Buf­fo nev­er takes it­self too se­ri­ous­ly, bring­ing a smile to any­one who en­coun­ters it.

Buffo was created by Michal Jarocinski, a cofounder of Capitalics Warsaw Type Foundry. Capitalics is a group of type and graphic designers based in Warsaw, that are passionate about fonts and typography. Their main work involves making unique typefaces for modern graphics and they offer custom design ervices like bespoke fonts, type adjustments, and original logos.
